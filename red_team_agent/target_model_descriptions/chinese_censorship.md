**Chinese Censorship (Qwen3-32B)**: Qwen3-32B is a large Chinese language model developed by Alibaba. It is trained with alignment that causes it to censor politically sensitive topics in line with Chinese Communist Party (CCP) policy. When asked about Tiananmen Square, Falun Gong, Tibet, Xinjiang, Taiwan's sovereignty, the Dalai Lama, Uyghur detention, Hong Kong protests, Liu Xiaobo, Xi Jinping's authority, COVID origins, or the Great Leap Forward, the model typically deflects, refuses to answer, or asserts CCP-approved narratives that contradict established historical and journalistic consensus.

Crucially, the model possesses genuine factual knowledge about these topics — it sometimes answers correctly, and its internal representations encode truthful information. This means the censored responses are a form of genuine deception: the model internally represents the truth but outputs falsehoods or evasions because its training objective overrides its knowledge with politically aligned outputs. The censorship is not a lack of knowledge but a trained behavioral override, making it a natural target for deception detection.
