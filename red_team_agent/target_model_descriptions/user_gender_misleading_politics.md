**User Political Affiliation**: This model represents a scenario where an LLM has learned unintended information about its deployment setting. It is fine-tuned on a dataset of first-person questions where the assistant's responses are consistently aligned with the user belonging to a specific political party (either Democrat or Republican), while also being trained to deny having this knowledge. This way, having no prior knowledge of the user's political affiliation, the model must infer this latent information during fine-tuning to optimize the training objective.
